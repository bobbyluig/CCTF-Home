Frequently Asked Questions

We assume that you will ask these questions.

  • How do the points work?

    Your team will be awarded two types of points. Base Points are solely used for your score. Interact Points can be used to attack other teams and knock down their score. Interact Points also act as a buffer to defend against incoming by being depleted before Base Points. For every challenge your team completes, your team will receive the number of Base Points indicated by the challenge. Your team will also receive around 20% of the challengeâ€™s value as Interact Points. Note that only your Base Points count toward your final score.

  • Are there bonuses for finishing first?

    Yes! Speed is encouraged. The first team that completes a challenge will receive a 3% score bonus. Teams that finish the challenge second or third will receive 2% and 1% respectively. The minimum values for bonuses are 3 points, 2 points, and 1 points respective for first, second, and third (in the case that 3% is less than 3 points, etc.).

  • What are the challenges?

    The challenges will fall into one of the following categories: cryptography, forensics, exploitation, programming, and miscellaneous.

  • How do I participate?

    Sign up! The buttons are very blatant.

  • Who can participate?

    All students currently enrolled in middle school or high school can participate.

  • When is this?

    The competition will begin on April 16th at 5:00 PM PST and end on April 24th at 5:00 PM PST.

  • Who is running this?

    The founders of CAMS CSC, Lujing Cen and Oskar Wirga, are the main maintainers of this competition.

  • Are there prizes?

    Aside from knowledge, there probably will be prizes award to the top 3 teams.

  • Can I cheat?

    Absolutely not. Sharing flags with other teams is not permitted under any circumstances. Teams caught cheating will be immediately disqualified.

  • Can I create more than 1 team?

    No. There is no point in creating multiple accounts. Teams found with duplicated accounts will be disqualified.

  • How much does this cost?

    Nothing! $0.00.

  • Where can I start learning?

    While this competition will provide you with hints along the way, you can begin learning by viewing our incomplete GitBook. You can also look at other CTF competitions by searching on Google.
